Mickey Mouse can be found in his resorts, located in several places around the world, where kids of all ages can play all day, meet with their favourite characters or even stay the night:

  • Disneyland Park

    Location: Anaheim, California, USA

    Opened: July 17, 1955

  • Walt Disney World

    Location: Lake Buena Vista, Florida

    Opened: October 1, 1971

  • Tokyo Disneyland

    Location: Urayasu, Chiba, Japan, near Tokyo

    Opened: April 15, 1983

  • Disneyland Paris

    Location: Marne-la-Vallée, France

    Opened: April 12, 1992

  • Hong Kong Disneyland

    Location: Penny’s Bay, Lantau Island, Hong Kong, China

    Opened: September 12, 2005

  • Shanghai Disneyland

    Location: Pudong, Shanghai, China

    Opened: June 16, 2016
