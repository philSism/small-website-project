Occupations

His numerous talents have allowed Mickey Mouse to occupy himself with plenty of jobs throughout the years. Some of his most notable jobs are listed below:

  • Sorcerer's Apprentice

    In one of his most famous roles in a segment of the 1940 film Fantasia, Mickey is the apprentice of the powerful sorcerer Yen Sid.

  • Peasant

    In the segment Mickey and the Beanstalk of the 1947 film Fun and Fancy Free Mickey is trying to live as a peasant together with his friends Donald and Goofy in the once beautiful (now turned wasteland) Happy Valley.

  • Musketeer

    After being rescued by three heroic musketeers as kids and then working as janitors for real musketeers, Mickey and his friends, Donald and Goofy, manage to become royal musketeers of the French throne in the 2004 classic Mickey, Donald, Goofy: The Three Musketeers.

  • Detective

    In many comic stories Mickey works as a detective, usually helping out Chief O'Hara and Detective Casey on solving cases and catching criminals such as Pete, The Phantom Blot and the like.
